Advance Excel Assignment 1

Q1. What do you mean by cells in an excel sheet?
solution: Cells in an Excel sheet are the individual rectangular boxes where data can be entered, stored, and manipulated. They are identified by their unique column and row labels.

Q2. How can you restrict someone from copying a cell from your worksheet?
solution: You can restrict copying cells from your worksheet by protecting the worksheet and setting permissions to disallow copying.

Q3. How to move or copy the worksheet into another workbook?
solution: To move or copy a worksheet into another workbook, right-click on the worksheet tab, select "Move or Copy," choose the destination workbook, and select whether to move or copy the worksheet.

Q4. Which key is used as a shortcut for opening a new window document?
solution: The shortcut key for opening a new window document in Excel is Ctrl + N.

Q5. What are the things that we can notice after opening the Excel interface?
solution: After opening the Excel interface,we can notice the ribbon with tabs such as Home, Insert, Page Layout, etc., a formula bar for entering and editing formulas, and the grid of cells where data can be entered.

Q6. When to use a relative cell reference in excel?
solution: Relative cell references in Excel are used when you want a formula to adjust automatically when copied to different cells. They change relative to the position of the cell containing the formula.
