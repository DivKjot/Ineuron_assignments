Advance Excel Assignment 5

Q1. How many types of conditions are available in conditional formatting on Excel?
Ans: There are three types of conditions available in conditional formatting in Excel: "Format Cells That Contain," "Format Cells Based on Cell Value," and "Format Cells Based on Formula."

Q2. How to insert border in Excel with Format Cells dialog?
Ans: To insert a border in Excel with the Format Cells dialog, select the cells you want to format, right-click and choose "Format Cells," go to the "Border" tab, select the desired border style, and click "OK."

Q3. How to Format Numbers as Currency in Excel?
Ans: To format numbers as currency in Excel, select the cells containing the numbers, right-click, choose "Format Cells," go to the "Number" tab, select "Currency" from the category list, and choose the desired currency symbol and other formatting options.

Q4. What are the steps to format numbers in Excel with the Percent style?
Ans: Steps to format numbers in Excel with the Percent style: 
Select the cells containing the numbers, right-click, choose "Format Cells," go to the "Number" tab, select "Percentage" from the category list, and adjust other formatting options as needed.

Q5. What is a shortcut to merge two or more cells in excel?
Ans: The shortcut to merge two or more cells in Excel is Alt + H + M .

Q6. How do you use text commands in Excel?
Ans: Text commands in Excel, such as those for formatting, alignment, or font changes, can be accessed through the "Home" tab on the ribbon. 
Simply select the text you want to format, then choose the desired command from the options available in the "Font," "Alignment," or other groups.
